Feedback from INTO Manchester 'Dr Stephen Lynch's talk on "Maths is fun" was extremely well delivered and proved to be highly popular with our students; there was standing room only for late comers. The audience found the presentation interesting and informative. Some memorable facts that students responded to were:- the concept that time varied in deep space they found astounding, the existence of non-Euclidean geometry was consider unsettling and others found fractals and there use in the modelling of the reproduction of flies inventive . The staff were fascinated by the process of obtaining an international patent. The entire audience was taken with AIBO; some students (and staff) regretted not taking up Dr Lynch's offer to play with the dog.'

'At the end of the lecture students were left with a better understanding of the impact that mathematics plays in the everyday world.
